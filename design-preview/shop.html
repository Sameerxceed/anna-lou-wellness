The Story

Emotional Support Jewellery is not just jewellery

Every piece in the ESJ collection is designed to be a physical anchor — something you can touch, hold, or see that connects you back to an intention you’ve set for yourself. It’s not about decoration. It’s about return.

Born from Anna’s coaching practice, each piece carries the energy of a specific crystal and is paired with a guided intention. When the world gets loud, your jewellery becomes your quiet reminder: I am here. I am safe. I am enough.

“I designed the first piece for myself during the hardest year of my life. I needed something to hold onto. That’s what ESJ is — something to hold onto.”
