Anna’s Story

From Portobello Market to Harrods. From luxury to healing. Beautifully Whole.

It started with a table at Portobello Market and a box of hand-stamped charms. Anna Lou of London was born from a belief that jewellery should mean something — that a tiny disc around your neck could carry a name, a date, a promise. What began as a weekend stall grew into a brand stocked in Harrods, Selfridges, Harvey Nichols, and even Disney. Celebrities wore it. Magazines featured it. The business scaled and the accolades followed. But somewhere between the wholesale orders and the press coverage, the woman behind the brand started to disappear.

“I built a business that the world could see. Then I had to find the woman it had buried.”

The conscious reset didn’t happen overnight. It came in waves — a divorce, a reckoning with burnout, a body that finally said enough. Anna stepped back from the machine she’d built and asked the question so many high-achieving women are terrified to ask: “Who am I without the performance?” The answer didn’t come from another business plan. It came from her body, from stillness, from the radical act of coming back to herself.

That return led to training. Deep, rigorous, embodied training. Anna became an ICF-accredited coach, a somatic trauma-informed practitioner, and certified in TRE (Tension & Trauma Release Exercises), Flash EMDR, Brainspotting, and IFS (Internal Family Systems). She didn’t collect credentials to decorate a wall — she earned them because she’d lived the work. Every modality she teaches, she’s walked through herself. CPD certified and constantly deepening her practice, Anna brings both professional rigour and raw personal experience to every session.

“Healing isn’t about becoming someone new. It’s about coming back to who you were before the world told you to be someone else.”

This is the philosophy behind Beautifully Whole and the Zen Hustler approach — the understanding that ambition and softness aren’t opposites. That you can build something extraordinary without losing yourself in the process. That strength isn’t the absence of vulnerability; it’s the courage to feel everything and keep going. Anna works with women who are done performing strength and ready to actually feel it. Through coaching, somatic healing, Emotional Support Jewellery, and a growing community of women on the same journey, Anna Lou Wellness is the space where the reset begins.

“You don’t need to be fixed. You need to come back to yourself.”
