Moonstone has been revered for centuries as a stone of inner growth and strength. It soothes emotional instability and stress, stabilising the emotions and providing calmness. In crystal healing, it is connected to the crown and third-eye chakras, enhancing intuition and promoting inspiration. This pendant is designed to sit close to the heart, serving as a daily reminder of your capacity for new beginnings.
Moonstone is a delicate crystal — handle with love. Avoid prolonged exposure to water or harsh chemicals. Cleanse energetically by placing under moonlight (especially during a full moon) or on a bed of selenite. Polish gently with a soft cloth. Store separately to avoid scratching.

The Story

Why this piece exists

The Moonstone Pendant was the first piece I designed after my own reset. I was coming out of a period where I’d lost connection with myself — my intuition was silent, my body was exhausted, and my mind was racing. I needed something physical to anchor me back.

Moonstone found me during that time. Its gentle energy reminded me that every ending carries a new beginning inside it. I wore a piece of raw moonstone in my pocket for three months before I designed this pendant. Every piece in the ESJ collection carries that same intention: to be your quiet companion when the world gets loud.

When you wear this pendant, you’re not just wearing jewellery. You’re wearing a promise to yourself — to keep returning, keep trusting, keep beginning again.
