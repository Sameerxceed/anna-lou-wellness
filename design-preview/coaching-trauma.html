From Anna

I know what it feels like to carry something you can’t name. The tightness in your chest that arrives without warning. The hypervigilance that disguises itself as being “on top of things.” The way you can hold an entire room together while quietly falling apart inside. I know, because I’ve been there. And I know that most of the world tells you to push through it, to be strong, to keep going. But your body is keeping score, even when you think you’ve moved on.

Healing isn’t about going back to who you were before. It’s about finally meeting who you’ve been underneath the armour.

Trauma release work isn’t talk therapy. It’s not about reliving every detail or performing your pain for someone else’s understanding. It’s body-first work — somatic, gentle, and profoundly effective. We work with your nervous system, not against it. Through TRE, Flash EMDR, Brainspotting, and IFS, we create space for your body to finally let go of what it’s been holding. The shaking, the tears, the relief — they come when your system feels safe enough to release. And I will be there, holding that space, every step of the way.

Modalities we work with

TRE

Trauma Release Exercises — guided somatic tremoring that helps your body discharge stored tension and stress.

Flash EMDR

A gentler form of EMDR that processes traumatic memories without full emotional re-exposure.

Brainspotting

Using eye position to access and process trauma held in the subcortical brain. Deep, focused, transformative.

IFS

Internal Family Systems — working with the different parts of yourself to find compassion, safety, and integration.

Domestic Abuse Recovery

Specialist support for women healing from coercive control, narcissistic abuse, and domestic violence.

Subconscious Healing

Accessing and reprogramming deep-seated beliefs that keep you stuck in survival patterns.

Pendulum & Belief Repatterning

Gentle muscle-testing and energetic tools to identify and shift limiting beliefs at the subconscious level.
