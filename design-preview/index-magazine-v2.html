Featured Reset Story

She Walked Away from Everything — and Found Herself at the Kitchen Table

By Anna Lou · 18 March 2026 · 8 min read

Lauren had spent fifteen years building a career that looked perfect from the outside. The corner office, the awards, the Instagram grid of conference stages and city skylines. But the morning she couldn’t get out of bed, she realised something had quietly broken. Not her body. Not her mind, exactly. But the story she’d been telling herself about who she was supposed to be.

This is the first in our series of Reset Stories — honest accounts from women who chose to come back to themselves, even when it meant letting everything else fall apart first.
