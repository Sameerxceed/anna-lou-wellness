## 1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the complete rebuild of **annalouwellness.com** as an integrated, multi-format wellness platform where **content, coaching, lifestyle, media, community, and commerce work together as one seamless commercial ecosystem** — not as separate sections.

The platform is designed for women who feel ready to step into a more aligned, elevated version of themselves. The experience will feel **aspirational, intuitive, and immersive** — drawing people in through editorial content, guiding transformation through coaching and workshops, and offering products as tangible extensions of that experience.

The design will be **adaptive and brand-led** — moving fluidly between editorial storytelling (Cup of Jo model), conversion-led coaching journeys (chriscorsini.com), clean commerce, immersive media, and community spaces. Each section has its own visual rhythm while staying unified under the Anna Lou Wellness brand identity (Brand Guidelines v1.0, April 2026).

We will deliver the **complete platform in a single 4-week build** with clear weekly milestones, using our proven ReactJS + Strapi technology stack.

## 2. Understanding the Vision

### The Client
Anna Lou is a somatic trauma-informed coach, ICF-accredited mentor, and founder of Anna Lou of London (jewellery brand sold at Harrods, Selfridges, Harvey Nichols, and internationally). She has consciously transitioned from luxury retail to coaching and healing work — and that transformation journey **is** the brand story. The brand tagline — **"Beautifully Whole"** — reflects the core ethos: integration of body, mind, spirit and life, and the return to self.

### The Core Ecosystem
This is not just a website — it is a **commercial platform** with multiple revenue streams, where every element supports a unified journey:

- **Content (blog/editorial + lifestyle)** draws people in and builds connection
- **Coaching and programmes** guide transformation and decision-making
- **Workshops** deliver paid group learning (£200 full / £15 summary access)
- **Media (YouTube + Podcast/Substack)** extends reach and builds authority
- **Products** act as tangible extensions of that experience
- **Community & membership** creates recurring revenue and engagement
- **Advertising & sponsorship** placements generate passive income

The user moves through the site organically — content naturally leads into coaching or products, and coaching pages function as **immersive, conversion-led journeys** rather than simple service listings. (Reference: chriscorsini.com/products/calling-in-love-2)

### Primary Audience
UK-based female founders, entrepreneurs, and leaders (30s–40s). High-achieving women experiencing burnout, nervous system overload, or the gap between external success and internal wellbeing. Spiritually curious, self-aware, ready to invest. Core geography: London and surrounding areas, with global reach via online programmes and memberships.

### Design Direction
The design is **not a single aesthetic** — it adapts to the purpose of each section while remaining cohesive under the brand identity:

- **Editorial pages** (blog, lifestyle) — warm, story-first, Cup of Jo-inspired editorial feel
- **Coaching & workshop pages** — immersive, conversion-led, long-form journeys
- **Shop pages** — clean, elevated commerce with story-connected product pages
- **Media pages** — hub-style layouts for video and podcast content
- **Community pages** — warm, inviting, participation-driven spaces
- **Landing/funnel pages** — focused, single-purpose conversion layouts
- All pages share the **Anna Lou Wellness brand identity**: crown logo, "Beautifully Whole" tagline, brand colour palette, and corporate typography
- Reference sites: Cup of Jo (editorial model), chriscorsini.com (coaching journeys), Goop, Mindbodygreen, Rose Inc

## 5. Technology Stack

| Layer | Technology | Why |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Frontend** | Next.js 15 (React) | Fast, interactive, SEO-optimised with server-side rendering |
| **CMS** | Strapi 5 (Headless) | Content editor-friendly, REST API, media management — Anna manages all content independently |
| **Mobile Admin** | Custom lightweight publisher | iPhone-first publishing interface — blog, reels, workshops in under 5 minutes |
| **Styling** | Tailwind CSS | Rapid theming with brand palette, responsive design |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL | Production-grade, scalable |
| **E-Commerce** | Shopify Buy Button | Embedded checkout on product pages. Shopify Starter handles payments, inventory, fulfilment. Customer never leaves annalouwellness.com |
| **AI** | Anthropic Claude API | Powers "Ask Anna" AI coaching assessment — personalised recommendations in Anna's voice |
| **Hosting** | Vercel (frontend) + Railway (CMS) | Auto-deploy, CDN, low cost |
| **Email** | Resend | Transactional emails, lead magnet delivery, notifications |

### Why ReactJS + Strapi (vs WordPress / Showit / Squarespace)

| Criteria | WordPress/Showit | ReactJS + Strapi |
|----------|-----------------|------------------|
| Performance | Plugin-heavy, slower | Optimised, fast-loading |
| Security | Plugin vulnerabilities | No server-side attack surface |
| Adaptive Design | Template-constrained | Fully custom layouts per section type |
| Integrated Ecosystem | Requires multiple plugins | One unified system |
| Membership & Community | Plugin-dependent | Custom-built |
| Monetisation (ads, tiers) | Limited | Fully customisable |
| CMS Experience | Complex admin | Clean, simple admin |
| Hosting Cost | £20–50+/month | ~£5–10/month |
| Scalability | Server-dependent | CDN-first, globally fast |
