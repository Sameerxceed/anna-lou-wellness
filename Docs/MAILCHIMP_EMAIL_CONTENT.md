# Mailchimp Email Content — Copy-Paste Cheatsheet

Source: `Docs/final docs/ALW_EMAIL_AUTOMATIONS_FINAL_Sameer.docx`

For each Mailchimp Customer Journey draft: open the journey, paste the Subject + Preheader into the email settings, paste the Body into the drag-and-drop builder (ALW Master template), then click Start to activate.

**Branding rules from Anna (apply to every email):**
- From: `Anna Lou` / `anna@annalouwellness.com`
- Sign-off: `Anna x` (lowercase x, no full stop)
- No em-dashes anywhere. Middle dots (·) are fine.
- Wordmark header on every email
